Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

YapiYapi Fun is designed for children and families. The site is a static browser game collection. It does not require an account, login, child name, email, school, photo, voice recording, chat, upload, purchase, or multiplayer profile.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not ask children for names, emails, phone numbers, precise location, photos, voice, or school information.
  • We do not offer chat, comments, social posting, user-generated uploads, or multiplayer messaging.
  • We do not sell virtual goods, subscriptions, or in-game purchases.
  • We do not create child profiles or send gameplay progress to our own server.

Game Data

Games may keep temporary progress such as levels, stars, stickers, missions, and scores in the browser. Solar System Doodle may use local browser storage for stickers and daily mission progress. This information is used only to run the games and is not submitted to our own server.

Parents' Choices

Because this site does not ask children to provide personal information, we do not maintain child account records for parents to review or delete. If you believe a child has provided personal information through an unexpected channel, contact us and we will review and delete it where applicable.
