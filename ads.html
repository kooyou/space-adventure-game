Ads

Advertising Policy

Space Adventure is child-directed. Ads must be reviewed and configured for children before launch. Do not enable live ads until the AdSense account, site domain, consent settings for the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, and child-directed ad treatment are correctly configured.

Allowed Ad Placement

  • Ads may appear only between levels, on policy pages, or below non-game content.
  • Ads must not cover the rocket, meteors, star fuel, knowledge cards, or gameplay controls.
  • Ads must be clearly separated from buttons such as Start, Pause, Restart, and Next.
  • No rewarded ad, forced ad click, deceptive ad label, or ad styled like a game reward.

Required Google Settings Before Launch

  • Use child-directed treatment for ad requests where required.
  • Use non-personalized ads for child-directed areas.
  • Use a Google-certified CMP where Google requires it, including EEA, UK, and Switzerland traffic.
  • Do not use remarketing, interest-based targeting, audience lists, or personalized ad selection for children.
  • Add your real AdSense publisher ID and domain only after account approval.
  • Publish a valid ads.txt file with your real Google publisher ID. Do not use a fake ID.
  • Review every ad category and block sensitive categories unsuitable for young children.

Current Site Status

This local version does not load Google ad scripts yet. That is intentional: live ad code should be added only after you have the final domain, AdSense publisher ID, and compliance settings ready.
