Parent Guide

Space Adventure is intended for ages 4-8. Children tap meteors, collect star fuel, and unlock simple knowledge cards about space and AI pattern learning.

Child Safety Design

  • No account, login, chat, comments, uploads, camera, microphone, or purchases.
  • Large tap targets and short levels for young children.
  • Ads, if enabled, should appear only between levels and never as gameplay controls.
  • Children should ask an adult before clicking external links or ads.

Learning Goals

  • Recognize planets, stars, comets, and meteors.
  • Learn simple AI concepts: examples, classification, confidence, and human guidance.
  • Practice observation: compare Moon and Earth, notice planet colors, and remember knowledge cards.
