Parent Guide

YapiYapi Fun is a small collection of browser learning games for children. Space Adventure and How Deep Is the Sea? are intended for ages 4-8, while Solar System Doodle is intended for ages 6-12. All games are designed for short, simple play sessions with no account or child profile.

Child Safety Design

  • No account, login, chat, comments, uploads, camera, microphone, or purchases.
  • Large tap targets and short levels for young children.
  • Progress may be kept locally in the browser for gameplay, but is not sent to our own server.
  • Ads, if enabled, should appear only between levels and never as gameplay controls.
  • Children should ask an adult before clicking external links or ads.

Learning Goals

  • Recognize planets, stars, comets, and meteors.
  • Learn simple AI concepts: examples, classification, confidence, and human guidance.
  • Explore the Sun and eight planets through missions, stickers, and quizzes.
  • Explore ocean depth, sea zones, submarines, and simple marine animal facts.
  • Practice observation: compare Moon and Earth, notice planet colors, and remember knowledge cards.
